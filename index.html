Dangerous Goods (DG) Transportation

The logistics industry has changed significantly over the past decade, with ever-increasing restrictions being placed on what you are able to ship. Dangerous goods, abbreviated DG, are substances that when transported are a risk to health, safety, property or the environment. Certain dangerous goods that pose risks even when not being transported are known as hazardous material.

Such as compressed oxygen or other gases, explosives, flammable liquids including alcohol and flammable solids, such as nitrocellulose, infectious substances, oxidizing substances, such as ammonium dichromate or pool chlorine, radioactive materials, perfume, aerosols and batteries are no longer accepted as standard carriage. These items are now only able to be shipped by specialists trained and qualified in Dangerous goods.

Our experienced team has attained all the necessary qualifications, and takes care of all the labeling and documentation required for your DG shipments.

Courier Syndicate provides international shipping solutions for consignments that are classified as Dangerous Goods. Whether you want to transport large volumes of DG goods, or an individual simply looking to send a bottle of perfume, we can help.
